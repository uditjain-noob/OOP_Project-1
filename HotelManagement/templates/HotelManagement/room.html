Delux Suite Bedroom

The Deluxe Suites are elegantly appointed two-room units, each with a separate sitting room and bedroom. The suites offer a beautiful view of the city.

Arched corridors lit with elegant lamps will lead you to our fine luxury suites. Step into an experience in opulence. Presidential Suite Bedroom

Situated at the top floor of the hotel, the Presidential Suite offers a breathtaking view of the city. It includes a cozy bedroom with a study-table. The ceiling of the bedroom is adorned with round lamps. The interiors are plush and the walls decorated with beautiful paintings.
